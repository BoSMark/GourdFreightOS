## Summary

Sustainability is not a feature GOURD Freight added: it is the founding reason the company exists. This document governs how GOURD Freight integrates carbon reduction into its service model and how it communicates and measures that work.

## Context

GOURD Freight was founded because the logistics industry wastes carbon through poor planning. The company's emissions reduction page is unusually specific and honest: it cites DfT data for rail vs road comparisons, acknowledges the carbon cost of AI compute, and offers a free Carbon Healthcheck delivered by accredited sustainability experts.

The company is on a journey towards B Corp certification. It actively works with "leading logistics sustainability advisors." The target market includes B Corp companies and any business with active carbon reduction goals.

## Core Principles

**1. Measure first.** Every freight quote includes carbon burn data for each movement. Clients see the carbon cost of their options before they choose. This is the baseline.

**2. Modal shift as the primary lever.** The company is explicit that road-to-rail modal shift is the single biggest carbon reduction lever available to most logistics clients. Rail provides up to 76% carbon reduction per container (DfT source). GOURD Freight actively promotes road-to-rail and road-to-river options.

**3. Re-use existing capacity.** "We do love a re-used asset, or an existing empty slot." Finding backhauls, spare warehouse slots, empty train carriages: exploiting embedded carbon before adding new capacity. This is a Kaizen-influenced approach to waste.

**4. Honest about AI's carbon cost.** The company states openly: "We acknowledge that [AI] has an impact on the carbon we burn, due to the compute-load of the servers required, but we use it to plan faster than a human can!" This transparency is a values statement, not a disclaimer.

**5. 'At the right time', not just 'in time'.** The company argues against reflexive Just-in-Time logistics when "at the right time" would prevent unnecessary movements. This is a position that requires educating clients, but it's the right one.

## What GOURD Freight Offers

- **Carbon reporting** on every shipment mode offered
- **Reduction suggestions** as part of every quote
- **Road to rail and river options** actively surfaced
- **Lower-carbon carrier options** surfaced during route planning
- **Carbon Healthcheck**: free 30-minute consultancy with an accredited sustainability expert; weekday office hours (Mon-Thu 15:00-16:30)
- **Ongoing sustainability consultancy** for clients who want a plan to reduce logistics carbon over time

The company references DfT (Department for Transport) methodology for carbon comparisons. The BIFA Carbon Calculator is available to members and provides a standard framework for calculating freight shipment emissions.
